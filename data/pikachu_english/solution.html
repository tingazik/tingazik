1. Convert the hex data into binary.
  2. Construct an audio file with the sample rate as stated, 1 channel and 1 bit depth.
  3. Fit the binary data as samples of the audio file. In case you cannot set bit depth to 1, polarized values will also work. (e.g. 00 for 0 and ff for 1 in 8-bit depth.
  4. Duration of the audio should match the value as stated.
  5. Play the audio file, and you will hear the answer.
